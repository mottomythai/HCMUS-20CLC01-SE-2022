Warranty Policy

All Hi-5 products will be covered for one year from date-of-purchase against issues arising from manufacturing defects and faults. This cover is only available to the original owner and is not transferable. Cover is only applicable to products when purchased new from an Authorised Hi-5 Dealer or Authorised Hi-5 Online Retailer.

Please be aware of circumstances that are not covered by our warranty:

The warranty period has expired.
Damage caused by general wear and tear, wear against abrasive surfaces such as rough seams, non-cycle specific clothing or under-saddle fenders.
Damage caused by UV (Sunlight or other natural phenomena), Fire, Bleach, Coloured Materials or Bicycle Cleaners.
Rips, Burns, Tears and Damages due to accident, normal wear and tear, improper care, mis-use, or the natural breakdown of colours, finishes and materials over time.
Damaged caused by improper repair, adjustments or lack of maintenance.
Proof of purchase is not presented.
